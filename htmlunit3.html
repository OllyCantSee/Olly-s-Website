Whistleblowing, and the legislation protecting individuals.

What is Whistleblowing?

Whistleblowing is the act of reporting or informing an organisation that a company has been engaging in unlawful or immoral activity.

Is whistleblowing a good thing?

Whistleblowing is considered a good thing! Whistleblowing is a key thing to have within a company as it supports a culture in which employees feel comfortable sharing thier concerns. This keeps moral high in a company as employees dont feel constrained.

What counts as whistleblowing?

Things that count as whistleblowing include: a criminal offence (for example fraud), someone's health and safety is in danger, risk or actual damage to the environment, a miscarriage of justice, a company is breaking the law (does not have the right insurance), someone is covering up wrongdoing. When reporting any of these these listed, you are protected by law!

What DOESN'T count as whistleblowing?

It is VERY important that people understand what DOESN'T count as whistleblowing. The most common mislabled whistleblowing is Personal grievances, this includes: Bullying, harassment or discrimination, these are still reportable but it is not covered by whistleblowing law, UNLESS your particular case is in the public interest.

Frances Haugen -

Frances Haugen disclosed tens of thousands of Facebooks internal documents to the securities and exchange commission. Frances claimed that the documents she leaked proved that Facebook repeatedly prioritised "growth over safety". She exposed Facebook for having many flaws and even took her concerns to court. Tyler Shultz -

Tyler Shultz revealed, accompanied by Erika Cheung, that Theranos (a health and technology company) had been altering data within their labs. Tyler Shultz and Erika Cheung fought hard against Theranos and managed to get the founder (Elizabeth Holmes) Fined and arrested.

Gamification In The Wokrplace.

What is Gamification?

Gamification is a powerful tool by which you make less enjoyable tasks/processes enjoyable by giving it attributes associated with playing a game. These attributes can include using a point system.

2 Examples Of Gamified Systems.

The first example of a gamified system was introduced within Omnicare. Omnicare was having big problems with customer service so sought it necessary to add game based elements to their employees work. Adding these gamified aspects (points and rewards) boosted moral within the workers and there was even a leaderboard created accompanied with rewards like amazon gift cards and cinema tickets.

The second example of a gamafied system was introduced into Fresh Desk. Fresh desk is a customer support service however was ladcing productivity so needed encouragement/motivation. Fresh Desk released the Fresh Desk Arcade, this "arcade" helped bring back the enjoyment within customer service and customer service became a rather than a chore.
